UTS Sequencing Facility

Located within the ithree institute, the UTS Sequencing Facility offers next generation DNA seqeucning using Illumina platform and bioinformatic services. Our aim is to provide affordable support for internal and external researchers, students and industries to generate genomic data.

{{"Our services"}}

Bacterial Whole Genome Sequencing

We offer a comprehensive DNA to bioproject pipeline. For only 80 AUD, you will get the sequencing data, the assembly of the bacterial genome and the free service for submitting your data onto the NCBI database with 1-year embargo. We do give discount for large scale samples.

16S Amplicon Sequencing

Services include:

  • QC genomic DNA

  • Library preparation

  • Sequencing on Miseq V3 2x300 bp

  • Minimum 80,000 reads per sample
