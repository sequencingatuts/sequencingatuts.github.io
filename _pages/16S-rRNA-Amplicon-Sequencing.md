<h1> 16S rRNA Amplicon Sequencing </h1>

The service includes:
- QC of the DNA
- library preparation
- Sequencing on MiSeq 2x300 bp
- A turnaround time of approximately 3-4 weeks after submission
- Minimum 80,000 reads per sample
